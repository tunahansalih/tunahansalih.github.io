## Summary
Ph.D. candidate at Virginia Tech, advised by Prof. Pinar Yanardag. My research focuses on understanding and controlling the internal representations of generative models, spanning controllable generation, diffusion model distillation, mechanistic interpretability, and flow matching. My work has appeared at CVPR, ICCV (Highlight), ICML (Oral), and AAAI. Before my PhD, I spent several years building production machine learning systems, and have interned at Waymo, Amazon, and Adobe during my PhD.
